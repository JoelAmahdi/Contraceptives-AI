Module 5 — Handled with Care

Sexual Activity History

🔒 Frequency & Partners

Sexual frequency, number of current partners, relationship type, use of barrier methods, perceived STI risk

⚡ Contraceptive Intent

Primary goal — pregnancy prevention, cycle regulation, symptom management (acne, pain), or dual purpose

💊 Prior Experience

Methods previously used, adherence challenges, side effects experienced, reasons for discontinuation

🩺 Partner Factors

Partner vasectomy, cooperation with methods, need for user-controlled discreet options, safety considerations

🛡️

Trauma-informed & culturally sensitive design: All questions are optional. The AI detects distress signals and responds with empathy. Partner safety concerns trigger immediate connection to a reproductive health specialist or counsellor — not just a protocol.

The Brain

The AI Decision Engine

📚 Knowledge Base

WHO MEC 5th Ed., ACOG guidelines, Cochrane reviews, pharmacological databases, 50,000+ clinical case patterns

⚡ Decision Model

Hybrid RAG-LLM with clinical decision tree overlay. Outputs ranked methods with match scores and contraindication flags

Every AI recommendation pathway is reviewed, validated & certified by a panel of board-certified OB/GYN and reproductive endocrinology specialists before deployment
